A Greener Tomorrow: Learn Environmental Sustainability

Whether you are 7 or 70, protecting our planet is a shared mission. It is Green Bridge Academy's goal to educate and empower no matter your age. Lets build a future where nature thrives and people live in harmony with the earth

What is Environmental Sustainability

Definition:Environmental Sustainability means using natural resources wisely so that future generations can enjoy clean air, fresh water, and a healthy planet.

Why it matters

  • We depend on nature for food, water, and shelter.
  • Pollution and waste harm wildlife and ecosystems.
  • Sustainable living ensures a better quality of life for everyone

The 3R's: Reduce, Reuse, Recycle

Reduce:Cut down on waste. Use only what you need. Choose productes with less packaging

ReuseFind new purposes for old items. Donate clothes, repurpose containers, and repair instead of replace.

Recycle:Sort paper, plastic, glass, and metal into recycling bins. Recycling saves energy and reduces landfill waste.

Caring for Nature and Wildlife

  • Avoid littering, trash harms animals and pollutes water.
  • Plant native flowers to support pollinators like bees amd butterflies
  • Learn about endangered species and support conservation efforts

Conserving Water and Energy

Water Savong Tips:

  • Turn the tap off while brushing your teeth
  • Collect rainwater for gardens
  • Fix leaks promptly

Energy Saving Tips:

  • Switch to LED bulbs.
  • Unplug devices when not in use.
  • Use natural light during the day.

Sustainable Transporttion

  • Walk, bike, or carpool when possible
  • Use public transport to reduce emissions.
  • Choose electric or hybrid vehicles if available.

Renewable Energy

What is Renewable Energy?It is energy from sources that naturally replenish such as, sunlight, wind, and water.

Type Source Benefit
Solar Power Sunlight Clean, abundant, and silent
Wind Power Wind No emissions, scalable
Hydro Power Flowing Water Reliable and efficient

Everyday Sustainable Habits

  • Bring reusable bags and bottles.
  • Compost food scraps.
  • Support local and eco-friendly businesses
  • Educate others, and share what you learn
Habit Impact
Using reusable bags Reduces plastic waste
